The delivery team will be asked questions during a service assessment.
These are examples of questions you might be asked. This is not an exhaustive list.

<details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
  <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
    <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
      User-centred design
    </span>
  </summary>
  <div class="govuk-details__text">
    <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
      <li>What constraints are you working within? What are the barriers to removing or reducing the impact of those constraints?</li>
      <li>How do you know a service is the right way to solve the problem you’re looking at?</li>
      <li>How do you know you’ve got the scope of the service right?</li>
      <li>How did you define the MVP?</li>
      <li>Talk us through a few of the design iterations you tested - and explain why you rejected them</li>
      <li>How will users find the service initially?</li>
      <li>Why do users need the thing you’re planning to build or buy?</li>
      <li>What do you know about the offline parts of your user’s journey?</li>
      <li>How will you know your service joins up with those offline elements?</li>
      <li>Who are you inviting to your user research sessions?</li>
      <li>What are you doing to make sure users don’t have to provide the same information multiple times and reusing data from across government?</li>
      <li>How are you planning to make sure that the service doesn't exclude any existing or potential users of your service?</li>
      <li>How are you improving users’ experience across different channels?</li>
      <li>How are you implementing recommendations from the Accessibility audit on your service?</li>
      <li>What is your approach to access needs and assisted digital needs?</li>
      <li>What support will you offer to users who have problems with the digital part of the service?</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</details>

<details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
  <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
    <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
      Team and agile ways of working
    </span>
  </summary>
  <div class="govuk-details__text">
    <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
      <li>Which roles are in your team?</li>
      <li>How are you engaging outside of your team? For example, inside or outside your department, or with the ops and policy professions?</li>
      <li>Has the team disagreed on anything and if so how was that resolved?</li>
      <li>How are you using agile methods?</li>
      <li>How does governance work? Are there any issues you’ve had to escalate?</li>
      <li>Did you discover anything that will make it difficult to design the service your users need? If so, what are your plans to address the issues you found?</li>
      <li>Is the team sustainable moving into public beta and beyond to live?</li>
      <li>What are you doing to make sure other teams know what you’re working on?</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</details>

<details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
  <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
    <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
      Service performance
    </span>
  </summary>
  <div class="govuk-details__text">
    <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
      <li>What key performance indicators (KPIs) have you identified so far, beyond the mandatory four?</li>
      <li>What existing data sources did you use to decide on your KPIs?</li>
      <li>Which metrics demonstrate your service is meeting your users’ needs?</li>
      <li>How will you check the service is doing what your department needs it to?</li>
      <li>What data are you collecting and how are you using it to improve your service?</li>
      <li>What management and governance do you have? (for example senior information risk owner (SIRO) approval, not collecting personal data, retained a raw backup data view)</li>
      <li>Which KPIs will you publish on data.gov.uk</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</details>

<details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
  <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
    <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
      Technology and security
    </span>
  </summary>
  <div class="govuk-details__text">
    <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
      <li>What security threats does the service perceive and what mitigations?</li>
      <li>Will the service be processing personal data?</li>
      <li>Does the service perceive any data protection issues either via sharing or hosting? For example, hosting outside the UK/EU.</li>
      <li>Has the service engaged the local data protection team and gone through the DPIA screening process?</li>
      <li>What is the plan for maintaining the security of the system and system dependencies?</li>
      <li>Is the service adhering to secure by design and are the secure by design artefacts being produced</li>
      <li>Is the service adhering to Data Protection by Design and by Default?</li>
      <li>Is the service to be assured via GovAssure and will the GovAssure assurance artefacts be produced</li>
      <li>Has the service been penetration tested and approved by DDTS Operational Security and relevant remediations made?</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</details>
