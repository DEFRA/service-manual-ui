Assessors typically work in product or delivery roles, but can come from any profession.

Required roles for an assessment panel:

- **Lead assessor** (chairs the assessment and submits the report)
- **User Researcher**
- **Technologist**
- **Performance Analyst**
- **Designer**

Join the **#ask-an-assessor** channel on the [cross-government Slack](https://ukgovernmentdigital.slack.com/archives/C0620J5U6) for support.

## Process

To become an assessor:

1.  Email [serviceassessments@defra.gov.uk](mailto:serviceassessments@defra.gov.uk)
2.  Observe 2 assessments
3.  Shadow 1 assessment with an experienced assessor
