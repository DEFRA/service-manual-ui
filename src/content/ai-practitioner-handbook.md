This handbook brings together guidance, patterns and lessons from across Defra to help you use AI safely and effectively.

It draws on work from the [AI Capabilities and Enablement team](https://github.com/DEFRA/ai-service-manual), the [AI SDLC Playbook](https://github.com/DEFRA/defra-ai-sdlc) and the [AI Modernisation Playbook](https://github.com/DEFRA/defra-ai-modernisation-playbook).

## How to use this handbook

Start with [Workflow](/ai-practitioner-handbook/workflow) to understand the development process, or jump to a specific section using the navigation.

If you are new to AI at Defra, read the [Getting started](#) sections first. If you are already using AI tools, the [Patterns and case studies](#) section shows what other teams have done and what they learned.

## What the AI Enablement team does

The team works across four areas:

- **Support** — helping teams adopt AI safely and effectively
- **Enable** — building reusable patterns, tools and prototypes
- **Explore** — testing emerging AI technologies through proofs of concept
- **Learn** — sharing lessons, case studies and prompts across the department
