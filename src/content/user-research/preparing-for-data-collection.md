## Before you start

Do these tasks before collecting data:

- **Data protection:** Complete training, screen for risks, and use approved tools (e.g. UR Vault).
- **Consent:** Prepare information sheets and forms. Participants must be able to withdraw anytime.
- **Analysis plan:** Decide who will analyse, where data goes, and how to share findings.

## During the session

### Observation and notes

- **Consent first:** Always get agreement before recording.
- **Be objective:** Record what happens, not just what you think.
- **Involve the team:** Invite observers to take notes.

### Storing data

Store all data in the **UR Vault** with restricted access.

- **Separate** personal data (consent forms) from research data.
- **Delete** local copies immediately after uploading.

## After data collection

### Anonymise everything

Remove names, faces, and contact details from all outputs (reports, slide decks).
