<p class="govuk-body-l">Defra user researchers:</p>

- plan research session logistics and adjust for users with accessibility needs
- prepare and brief observers and note-takers before sessions
- share observation and data capture templates to support consistent note-taking
- share analysis frameworks to structure and interpret research data
