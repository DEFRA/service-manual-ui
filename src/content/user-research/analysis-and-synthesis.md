<p class="govuk-body-l">Defra user researchers:</p>

- give observers and note-takers clear observation and data capture templates before research sessions
- use shared analysis frameworks to structure and interpret research data
- use shared synthesis artefacts to build a consistent, team-wide understanding of insights
