Defra user researchers adopt a wide range of user research methods and techniques. Research method and techniques guidance and templates are listed below.

## Research method guidance and templates

- Desk research
- Depth interviews
- Contextual observations
- Focus groups/ workshops
- Pop-up research (guerilla research)
- Usability testing
- Prototype testing
- Online surveys
- Live service desk research
- Live service performance tracking
- Research with users with accessibility needs

## Research technique guidance and templates

- User journey mapping
- Card sort
- Tree testing
- A/B testing
- Empathy mapping

## Standards

All research must comply with Defra’s user research standards and guidance.
