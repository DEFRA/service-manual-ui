Defra user researchers use a wide range of channels to communicate, collate and store research. Guidance and templates are listed below.

## Communicating research findings

- Research playbacks
- Show and tells
- Product roadmap and backlog planning
- DDTS show the thing
- Digital town hall
- Conferences

## Collate and share strategic insights across your service area

Defra user researchers input user needs into the data and insight management tool, and input user research insights and reports into the research libraries. The process to follow is listed below.

- Data and insight management process (user needs)
- Research libraries

## Storing research safely and securely

All research sharing and storage must comply with Defra’s user research standards and guidance.
