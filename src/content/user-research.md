## User researcher journey at Defra

Defra user researchers:

- follow a clear [onboarding](https://defra.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/def-ddts-portfoliohub/IQDctxVZRadZQI19wmkltRN3AX8dRq6bcYxIBQa7iyWGxEQ?e=jar3o1) process
- use the [community contact list](https://defra.sharepoint.com/:x:/s/def-ddts-portfoliohub/IQATgI6d-M7ISKCugyVWPqRfAULhLZH7RLeZdkP8nA2W2Nk?e=elXdv5) to connect with the community
- follow a clear [offboarding](https://defra.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/def-ddts-portfoliohub/IQAvdYQlNKvNS52IW6Uf70dbATjrOzwxvpl2Pk8Qt7b0JT8?e=XdUmPQ) process and complete the [handover template](https://defra.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/def-ddts-portfoliohub/IQAMkbv1LlcsTpf-DORvL45iAdV0ZJXcp1HBrYcBerXfJvo?e=lYUMHn)
- contact resource managers for additional support at [user-researchresourcemanagers@defra.gov.uk](mailto:user-researchresourcemanagers@defra.gov.uk)

## Research process

Defra user researchers:

- run scoping workshops to build a shared understanding of the problem
- create and agree research plans so research meets programme and stakeholder needs before delivery starts
- recruit a diverse and representative range of users to help make sure services work for all users
