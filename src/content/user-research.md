## Standards, guidance and tools

Defra user researchers follow recognised user research standards, use approved tools and work with their teams to make sure services meet the GDS Service Standard. These are listed below:

- [Standards and guidance](/user-research/standards-and-guidance)
- [Tools](/user-research/tools)

## Research process

Defra user researchers run scoping workshops and create research plans to build a shared understanding of the problem and ensure research meets programme and stakeholder needs before delivery starts.

Research plans are mandatory for all DDTS user researchers.

Defra user researchers also make sure services work for all users by involving a diverse range of people in research and design.

The process and templates are listed below:

- [Research scoping and planning](/user-research/scoping-research)
- [Participant recruitment](/user-research/recruiting-participants)
