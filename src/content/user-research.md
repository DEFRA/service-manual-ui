## Follow Defra’s user research standards and use Defra tools

Defra user researchers follow recognised user research standards and work with their teams to make sure services meet the GDS Service Standard.

Research must meet the standards and guidance listed below:

- [Standards and guidance](/user-research/standards-and-guidance)
- [Tools](/user-research/tools)

## Follow Defra’s processes, guidance and templates at every stage of the research lifecycle

Defra user researchers run scoping workshops and create research plans to build a shared understanding of the problem and ensure research meets programme and stakeholder needs before delivery starts.

Research plans are mandatory for all DDTS user researchers. The process, guidance and templates are listed below:

- [Research scoping and planning](/user-research/scoping-research)
- [Participant recruitment](/user-research/recruiting-participants)
