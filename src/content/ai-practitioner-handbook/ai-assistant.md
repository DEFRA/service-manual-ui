The AI assistant is a proof of concept built by the AI Capabilities and Enablement team. It gives Defra teams a safe and secure environment to experiment with advanced AI techniques.

## What it does

The assistant provides a controlled platform where teams can interact with large language models without exposing sensitive data to public services. It supports common tasks such as drafting content, analysing documents, and exploring technical questions.

## What it enables

Teams can use the assistant to:

- test AI approaches before committing to a full implementation
- explore how different prompting strategies affect output quality
- build confidence with AI tools in a governed environment
- share findings and patterns with other teams across Defra

## How it works

The assistant connects to approved AI models through secure infrastructure managed by the team. Access controls ensure that data stays within Defra's security boundary. Usage is logged for audit and learning purposes.

## Current status

This is an active proof of concept. The team continues to develop new features and gather feedback from users across Defra. Contact the AI Capabilities and Enablement team if you want to try it or provide feedback.
