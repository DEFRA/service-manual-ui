A legacy service was replatformed onto new CDP cloud infrastructure with AI assistance. The team completed the migration in 25% of the originally estimated time — a 75% reduction.

## Key result

The replatforming was completed 75% faster than the original estimate. AI tooling helped the team understand the existing codebase, generate migration scripts and accelerate testing.
