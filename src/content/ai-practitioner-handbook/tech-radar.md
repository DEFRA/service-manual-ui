The AICE Technology Radar maps AI tools, frameworks, techniques and platforms. It gives teams a clear view of what has been evaluated and what is safe to use.

## Status levels

Each item on the radar sits in one of 4 levels:

- **Endorse** — approved for use by delivery teams across Defra
- **Pilot** — being tested with specific teams under controlled conditions
- **Assess** — under evaluation by the AICE team only, not for wider use yet
- **Do Not Use** — avoid for security, licensing or compliance reasons

## Categories

The radar organises items into 4 categories:

- **Tools** — individual AI tools and applications
- **Platforms** — hosting, orchestration and infrastructure platforms
- **Techniques** — approaches and methods for using AI effectively
- **Frameworks** — libraries, SDKs and development frameworks

## How the radar is maintained

The AICE team reviews and updates the radar regularly. New tools are assessed as they become available. Existing entries move between levels based on team feedback and evaluation results.

## Give feedback

If you have experience with a tool on the radar, or want to suggest a new one, contact the team through the **#ask-ace** Slack channel.
