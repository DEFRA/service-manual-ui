What Defra teams have done with AI in real delivery work, and what they wish they had known earlier. Around 200 people across 20 teams have used AI on live projects since the pilot began in March 2025.

## Case studies

Real outcomes from teams across the AI pilot, with measurable results.

- [Nature Restoration Fund Discovery](/ai-toolkit/case-studies/nrf-discovery): 56% faster research synthesis on a 12-week discovery.
- [Packing List Parser cycle time](/ai-toolkit/case-studies/plp-cycle-time): 61% faster delivery cycle with GitHub Copilot.
- [Nature Restoration Fund Alpha](/ai-toolkit/case-studies/nrf-alpha): one of Defra's highest assessment scores after an AI-enabled discovery.

## Lessons learned

What teams wish they had known earlier.

- [Polished AI code can hide problems](/ai-toolkit/lessons-learned/code-quality): review AI output for maintainability, not just correctness.
- [Set clear rules to avoid shadow IT](/ai-toolkit/lessons-learned/governance): without clear governance, teams use unapproved tools.
- [Always validate AI outputs](/ai-toolkit/lessons-learned/output-validation): well-designed AI content can hide inaccurate data.
