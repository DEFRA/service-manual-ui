This guidance is for Defra digital practitioners working with AI in their day-to-day roles. That includes engineers, designers, user researchers, business analysts, product managers, delivery managers and content designers.

It draws on what teams have done across the Defra AI pilot programme: around 200 people across 20 teams using AI in real delivery since March 2025.

## Get started

If you're new to using AI in your role, start here. These pages cover the mindset, the building blocks and how to set up your project safely.

- [Welcome to AI at Defra](/ai-toolkit/guidance/welcome): who this guidance is for and how to use it.
- [Choosing models](/ai-toolkit/guidance/choosing-models): how to pick the right model for the task.
- [Working mindset](/ai-toolkit/guidance/working-mindset): treat AI as a collaborator, not a replacement.
- [The four pillars](/ai-toolkit/guidance/four-pillars): what you need in place to get consistent results.
- [Setting up your project](/ai-toolkit/guidance/setting-up-your-project): install your assistant, turn on privacy, add rules files.
- [Training and resources](/ai-toolkit/guidance/training-and-resources): vendor learning hubs and Defra training.

## Working with AI

Practical guides for the day-to-day work of building with AI. These cover the workflow, prompting, requirements and how to keep your repository consistent.

- [The AI development workflow](/ai-toolkit/guidance/workflow): from idea to deploy with AI in the loop.
- [Writing good prompts](/ai-toolkit/guidance/writing-good-prompts): context, role, tasks, constraints and how to refine.
- [Generating requirements](/ai-toolkit/guidance/generating-requirements): using AI for user stories, PRDs, data models and architecture.
- [Feature development with AI](/ai-toolkit/guidance/feature-development): agent-mode workflow, test-driven development, refactoring.
- [Rules for AI in your repo](/ai-toolkit/guidance/rules-for-ai): Copilot, Cursor, Claude Code and Windsurf conventions.
- [MCP servers and integrations](/ai-toolkit/guidance/mcp-servers): using approved MCP connectors safely.
- [Cost and tokens](/ai-toolkit/guidance/cost-and-tokens): what AI-assisted work actually costs.

## Responsible AI

How to use AI responsibly at Defra. Covers ethics, security, sustainability, data classification and personal data handling.

- [Ethics](/ai-toolkit/guidance/ethics): accountability, bias and public trust.
- [Security](/ai-toolkit/guidance/security): protecting code, secrets and your development environment.
- [Sustainability](/ai-toolkit/guidance/sustainability): using AI in proportion to the task.
- [Information governance](/ai-toolkit/guidance/information-governance): OFFICIAL classification rules and data protection.
- [PII and data handling](/ai-toolkit/guidance/pii-and-data-handling): what to redact before any AI sees it.
