AICE is working with the Defra Design community to build plugins that surface the AICE pattern library inside designers' existing tools, so reusable patterns are one click away rather than buried in a microsite.

## Who it helps

Defra interaction designers get fast access to vetted AICE patterns without leaving their design tools, which cuts search time and increases reuse.

The wider Defra community gets a shared distribution channel for patterns, plus a feedback loop from the Design community that shapes what AICE builds next.

## What we're doing

- Building and demoing the plugin with the Defra Design community
- Shaping the roadmap from what designers ask for
