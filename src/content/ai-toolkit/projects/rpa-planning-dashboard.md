AICE will support the Rural Payments Agency Discovery team to replace an Excel, Access and VBScript planning system with Power Platform, using AI to accelerate the build where it adds clear value.

## Who it helps

The Rural Payments Agency planning team gets a route to a modern, self-service planning capability with integrated dashboards, replacing manual chasing across 200 to 300 milestone owners.

The wider Defra community gets a worked example of when to use AI for build acceleration and when to keep deterministic tooling in the path of approval and core decisions.

## What we're doing

- Bounded consultation on AI-assisted development for the rebuild team
- Pointers to Microsoft Copilot patterns for interpreting VBScript and Access logic
- Light review of any in-product AI ideas that surface during the build
- Helping the team route into Defra's Power Platform community of practice
- Capturing reusable patterns for similar legacy modernisation work elsewhere in Defra
