AICE is working with the Rural Payments Agency scheme guidance team to measure the cost of fragmented guidance, consolidate it, and prepare the foundations for AI retrieval when staff need it.

## Who it helps

The Rural Payments Agency scheme guidance team gets a route to a single, well-governed content base that call handlers and scheme advisers can trust under pressure, with fewer escalations and less time lost chasing the right answer.

The wider Defra community gets a reusable pattern for measuring, consolidating and tagging an internal guidance estate before adding AI on top.

## What we're doing

- Measuring the cost of fragmented guidance
- Consolidating and tagging the content so it is ready for AI
- Sharing what we learn as a reusable pattern
