AICE will spend four to six weeks building a proof of concept with the Service Insights team, answering recurring service questions in plain English over a curated slice of real service data.

## Who it helps

The Service Insights team gets a working prototype that shows what good looks like to senior leaders, rather than describing it in slides. Foundations for ownership and refresh are settled alongside the build.

The wider Defra community gets an early flagship for the AI accelerator approach, including retrieval patterns over structured service data that other teams can reuse.

## What we're doing

- Agreeing the two or three priority service questions the prototype will answer
- Identifying five to ten priority users to test with
- Curating a seed data slice the prototype can be grounded on inside two weeks
- Building together over four to six weeks, with twice-weekly joint sessions and a mid-point demo
- Bringing the wider Defra AI and Microsoft Copilot direction in early, so the prototype fits
