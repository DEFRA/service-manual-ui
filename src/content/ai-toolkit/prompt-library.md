Tried and tested prompts shared by delivery teams to help you get the most out of AI tools.

The prompt library is a place for people across Defra to share the best of what they are doing with prompts. It is not a search-and-copy resource. The prompts are designed to show effective approaches that you can adapt to your own work.

## What you will find

The library contains three types of content.

**Prompts** are standalone instructions you can use with any AI assistant. They cover common tasks like converting meeting transcripts into user stories or structuring research findings.

**Workflows** are multi-step processes that combine several prompts in sequence. For example, the AI prototyping workflow takes you from design screenshots through to a working prototype.

**Repositories** are collections of agent rules and skills for AI coding assistants like [GitHub Copilot](/ai-toolkit/tools/github-copilot), Cursor and Claude Code. These help configure your tools to follow Defra standards automatically.

## How to contribute

If you have a prompt, workflow or set of agent rules that has worked well for your team, you can submit it to the library. Sharing what works helps other teams get started faster and avoids people solving the same problems independently.

Email the AI Capability and Enablement team at [AICapabilityAndEnablement@defra.gov.uk](mailto:AICapabilityAndEnablement@defra.gov.uk) to submit a contribution.
