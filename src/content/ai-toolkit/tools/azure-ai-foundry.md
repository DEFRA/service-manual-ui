Azure AI Foundry is Microsoft's platform for enterprise AI operations. It brings model deployment, fine-tuning and agent development into a single managed service, with access to a wide range of models from Microsoft, OpenAI, Meta and others, through a web portal at [ai.azure.com](https://ai.azure.com/) and an SDK.

## Why we are trialling it

A small number of Defra teams are using Foundry in real delivery, with support from the AI Capability and Enablement team. The team is collecting evidence on cost, performance and integration before recommending wider use.

Talk to the team before using it in delivery. They can advise on tenancy, model choice and the controls you need in place.

## When to consider it

Foundry may be a good fit if you need:

- access to OpenAI and other models through Azure
- to build and evaluate AI agents with managed tooling
- to keep data inside Azure, where your service already runs
