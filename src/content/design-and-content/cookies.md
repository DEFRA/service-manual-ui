To comply with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), you must manage how your service uses cookies.

## Your responsibilities

You must:

- tell users if your site uses cookies or similar technologies
- clearly explain what the cookies do and why
- not store cookies for longer than necessary (max 1 year)
- get explicit consent before storing non-essential cookies
- save consent preferences for 1 year only
- not store unique identifiers in the consent preference cookie

## User rights

A user must be able to:

- withdraw consent as easily as they gave it
- change their cookie settings at any time
- use the service without consenting to cookies
