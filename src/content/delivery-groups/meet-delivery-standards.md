These standards ensure delivery groups have clarity on their outcomes, understand their current services, have clear roadmaps for change and methods to measure the impact of change.

## 1. Define and share outcomes

Set a small number of clear outcomes and make sure everyone understands them.

[Read more about point 1](/delivery-groups/meet-delivery-standards/define-outcomes)

---

## 2. Maintain a list of products and services

Maintain a clear, up-to-date list of all your services and who owns them.

[Read more about point 2](/delivery-groups/meet-delivery-standards/products-and-services)

---

## 3. Publish a roadmap for change

A clear, prioritised roadmap showing how you'll evolve to deliver better outcomes.

[Read more about point 3](/delivery-groups/meet-delivery-standards/roadmap-for-change)

---

## 4. Define success measures and share progress

Define and share clear measures of success so everyone can see whether outcomes are being achieved.

[Read more about point 4](/delivery-groups/meet-delivery-standards/success-measures)
